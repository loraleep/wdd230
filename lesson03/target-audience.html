Target Audience

My audience consists of people who live in Boise who are more establish in their careers so ages 30-50 and making between $45k-$145k. They are interested in learning about local businesses, networking, socializing and like being part of a community

Andrea Walker

Andrea is 34, she works as an actuary in downtown Boise and is looking for ways to meet new people in Boise, as well as discover local businesses.

  1. What are some businesses new to Boise?
  2. Where can I meet people in my area?
  3. Where are the events happening in Boise?
  4. I want to buy some new furniture, but I want to shop local
  5. I want to get to know more people in my community

Andrew Parker

Andrew is 39 and living in Southeast Boise working as a softare security engineer and wants to network with people, hoping to start some freelance work as a side hustle.

  1. Where can I go to market myself?
  2. Are there any businesses that are looking to hire?
  3. Where can I meet people in my same profession?
  4. I want to do more freelance, are there any events that could help me meet people?
  5. I think it would be good to get out of the office every now and then
